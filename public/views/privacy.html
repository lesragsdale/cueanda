Like many websites, we use, or may use, "cookie" technology to collect additional website usage data and to improve our Service. A cookie is a small data file that is transferred to your computer's hard disk. Cueanda may use both session cookies and persistent cookies to better understand how you interact with our Service, to monitor aggregate usage by our users and web traffic routing on our Services, and to customize and improve our Service. Most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies. You can instruct your browser, by changing its settings, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit. However, some Services may not function properly if you disable cookies.
Our servers automatically record information created by your use of the Service. Such data may include information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, the referring web page, pages visited, location, search terms, and cookie information. We may receive such data when you interact with our Service, such as when you visit and sign into our website, or interact with our email notifications. These third-party service providers may collect information sent by your browser as part of a web page request, such as cookies or your IP address.
